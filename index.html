Experience

Over the past two years, I have taught over 500 hours of Economics & Maths lessons at both GCSE and A-Level, as well as preparing students for the 11+ exam. During this time I have become proficient in using online classrooms to ensure students get the most out of their time with me.

I pay close attention to each student's respective exam board. I dedicate time to explore the specification / mark schemes and adapt lessons to ensure the content and techniques I teach are specific to the requirements of the student's relevant exam board.

I provide thoroughly planned yet flexible lessons, identifying which skills students need to develop, providing well-explained examples, and using plenty of practice questions.

Why we are the Right Tutors for You

Economics: I have specialist knowledge of the essay technique required to succeed in the Economics exams, furthered by three years spent learning from an exam board member. I tailor lessons to each student’s respective exam board and have comprehensive notes for each one.

Maths: I have experience supporting students across all the major exam boards, and at all levels of ability. Whether Maths is your passion, a supplement to Economics, or you just want to pass the exam, I have helped students achieve their individual goals with great success.

11+ exam: Having benefitted from tuition for the 11+ myself, I understand the difficulties of the exam and the value a tutor can provide in helping you secure a place at your desired school.
